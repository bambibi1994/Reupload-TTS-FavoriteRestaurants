This is my Favorite Food

Chinese Roast Pork

This is my favorite food because you can eat it by itself or with anything else

without losing it taste. Chinese roast pork also is the food that I use to judge

any asian restaurant skill in cooking and select ingredients. It is one of two food

that we always have when have party in house alongside with eggroll. With correct

ingredients and cooking skill, you will feel cripy skin, melting fat, and tasteful

meat in your mouth in one bite. I Always recommend it to any non-asian people when

it comes to asian cuisine.
